WHAT IS
WCUP NATION?

WCUP NATION is not just a token; it's the digital home for the world's greatest sport. We are building a borderless community where the passion of the World Cup meets the transparency of Web3.

Inspired by the 48 teams that will make history in the USA, Canada, and Mexico, our mission is to create a decentralized legacy that unites fans from every corner of the globe.
