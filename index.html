1. Introduction

BEYOND THE
CHAIN.

WCUP is a community-driven meme coin built on the BNB Chain, created with a lighthearted spirit but a clear purpose: to promote unity among people and cultures through football.

Anywhere in the world, a ball rolling can instantly create bonds between people who have never met before. We play on the same team.

Connecting 5 Continents

The Vision

Building an inclusive global community using memes, creativity, and blockchain technology to bring people together from America to Oceania.

The Values

Encouraging respect, joy, diversity, and cooperation. Promoting sportsmanship as the core of our digital stadium.
